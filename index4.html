Sickle cell disease


We study sickle cell disease from the perspective of blood cell biology and tissue-specific interactions. By combining functional genetics, ex vivo cultures, and multi-omic analyses (single-cell, ATAC-seq, proteomics), we aim to identify target mechanisms and accelerate translation towards new therapeutic strategies.
